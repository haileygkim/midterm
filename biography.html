Cho Gi-Seok is a Korean artist, specializing in portraiture photography. He is known for his surrealistic, dream-like style with signature features such as extravagant props and bold makeup. As a homage to his culture and heritage, his work explores Korean identity with an exclusive usage of Asian models.

"I was thinking about beauty that can be engineered. I like the beauty that comes from the harmony of opposites. I thought about what factors would make up a person today. I thought about the surrounding nature and current technologies together, and tried to express these qualities through a human form."
